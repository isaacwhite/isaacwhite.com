Welcome to Gesture Video!

Gesture video let's you watch a video using the HTML5 video tag and contol it with gestures. For this demo, we've loaded up the trailer for Tron Legacy. The application has 11 gestures that let you control basic aspects of playback:

  • Play/pause (click)
  • Step forward
  • Step backward
  • Volume up
  • Volume down
  • Increase speed
  • Decrease speed
  • Mute
  • Full volume
  • Increase size
  • Decrease size

The entire window may be used to draw these gestures. It doesn't matter how big the video is zoomed to; the gesture area is always the full window size.
